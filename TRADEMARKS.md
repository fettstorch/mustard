The Mustard name and mustard-bottle logo identify the official project and
releases maintained by @fettstorch.dev. The project's software license does not
grant rights under trademark law to use these marks.

## Uses that do not require permission

You may:

- redistribute an unmodified official Mustard release with its original name
  and branding;
- truthfully describe a work as being based on, compatible with, or built for
  Mustard; and
- use the name to discuss or contribute to the project.

These uses must not imply sponsorship, endorsement, or official status.

## Modified versions

Permission to brand a modified version or fork as Mustard is not granted.
Modified versions must use a different product name and logo unless
@fettstorch.dev gives prior written permission. They may still truthfully state
that they are based on Mustard.

Nothing in this policy restricts rights that applicable trademark law grants
without permission.
